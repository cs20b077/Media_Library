Mahesh Babu, popularly known as Prince, is one of the reigning young stars of Telugu cinema. Mahesh was born on 9th August 1974 to actor Krishna in Chennai. He is one of five siblings. He has an older brother Ramesh, two older sisters Padmavathi and Manjula and a younger sister Priyadarshini.
